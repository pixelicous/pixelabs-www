Details

In 2014 I visited Venice, Italy. I immediately took notice like any tourist at the masks they have.

There is a big notion there to just custom the masks, make them steampunk etc, so I decided I'll do my own take on it

I went into a few stores, bought all sorts of weird supplies and started sketching. I quickly decided to just go with the flow.

Through the images below you can see the painting process, It involved a total of around 20 layers give or take.

Other points

  • Custom printed PCB (with OSHPark).
  • 12v EL Inverter, 8 channel EL wire driven with SparkFun EL Escudo Dos shield.
  • 3 blue LED lights
  • Sound activated with sensitivity control
  • Potentiometer and switch control for modes
