Details

The crafts here are accessories with electronics enabled for Burning Man event of 2018. Most of the items I created are ran by some sort of Arduino iteration. And as burning man is devoted to the act of giving, I basically ended up giving most of them as gifts. :)

The led themselves are Adafruit's neopixels, crazy bright, I had to dial them down so I won't blind people.

The NeoPixels LEDs have Integrated Drivers and their own micropython library, you can find my code online in my GitHub repositories.

In comparison with computer software, the harder part with coding lightshow with electronics, is that you can easily burn them. Driving the wrong value or connecting the wrong pin can has disastrous consequences. Debugging and trial and erroring was also very hard, between your vision of how to express specific light show as code, to what actually happens and then fixing it was a long learning process.

Ski Goggles + mask parts:

  • 2 * Adafruit Trinket M0
  • NeoPixel Stick - 8 x 5050 RGB LEDs
  • Lithium Ion Polymer Battery - 3.7v 150mAh
  • JST-PH Battery Extension Cable - 500mm

Silver/Golden "Unicorn Battle Goggles" Parts list:

  • Adafruit Trinket - Mini Microcontroller - 5V Logic
  • 2 * NeoPixel Ring, 12 x 5050 RGB LEDs

Pendant Parts list:

  • Adafruit GEMMA M0
  • NeoPixel Ring - 12 x 5050 RGB LEDs
  • NeoPixel Jewel - 7 x 5050 RGB LEDs

LED Pin:

  • 8x8 Red LED Matrix using MAX7219CNG chip to interface Arduino.
