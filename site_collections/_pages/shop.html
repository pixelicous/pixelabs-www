Shipping Rates

Shipping cost will be added automatically at checkout. The shipping rate is calculated based on item and destination, using the Standard shipping method.
The prices are grouped by product category, so all items within a single category have the same shipping fee. Several items of the same category delivered in the same order will have an additional, lower fee.
For example - the shipping rate for a t-shirt is 4$. Every additional t-shirt or item from the same category in that order adds 1.3$ to the shipping cost.

* Our prices do not include any additional cost/tax you might have at the destination address' country.
* Some larger variants such as sizes 2XL or bigger bags can be up to $1.50 extra per item. Additional details will be writte in the item description.
